Our Story

Hi, I'm the founder of Speedy Sauces, Clark Bolin. You might be wondering where the name comes from, and it's derived from my nickname, Speedboat. I got it because I worked on the boat pictured on this page. On the boat, we had limited food options, so I turned to hot sauce to relieve me from the monotony. I really began to appreciate how spice can elevate food. Hot sauce became my passion, and through this online shop, I hope to bring you a selection of hot sauces that will make your taste buds sizzle and your face smile!
